Tutorial: How to Play the Piano

Steps to Play

  1. Press the white keys to play natural notes (e.g., C, D, E).
  2. Press the black keys for sharps or flats (e.g., C#, D#).
  3. Explore preloaded songs using the dropdown menu.
  4. Practice with the "Key Assist" feature to learn notes visually.
  5. Use the "Metronome" to maintain rhythm.

Tips for Beginners

  • Start with simple songs like "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star."
  • Learn to play scales (C major, G major, etc.).
  • Practice daily to build muscle memory.
